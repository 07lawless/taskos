Eat the frog

Your daily priority system

The Frog Task is your single most important task of the day. Mark it as your frog and it gets pinned at the top of the board with green styling, a frog icon and priority labelling — so it's impossible to ignore.

🎯
One clear focus
Marking a task as the frog removes uncertainty about what to do first. You start every session knowing exactly where to begin.
✅
Beat procrastination
The method encourages you to tackle your hardest or most valuable work before smaller distractions. Everything else gets easier once the frog is done.
"If it's your job to eat a frog, it's best to do it first thing in the morning." — Mark Twain

Collaboration Board

Plan and assign work as a team

Every team gets its own shared Collaboration Board — a Kanban board separate from your personal workspace where any member can create tasks and assign them to teammates. Open it from the Collaborate tab.

🗂️
Shared To Do / In Progress / Done
One board per team with the same three-column workflow as your personal board, visible to every member.
🙋
Assign to anyone
When creating or editing a task, pick any teammate from the Assign To dropdown — or leave it unassigned for anyone to pick up.
🔔
Assignment notifications
Assigning or reassigning a task to someone sends them a notification, which appears live in the bell menu within 30 seconds.
🔀
Multiple teams
If you belong to more than one team, a team switcher at the top of the Collaboration Board lets you jump between each team's board.
📊
Team Analytics (admins only)
Team admins get an Analytics dashboard for each team: headline stats (total, completed, in progress, overdue, completion rate and average time-to-done), a bar chart of tasks completed per member, an eight-week throughput trend, a status split, and a per-member breakdown table. Average completion time is measured from task creation to completion, so tasks completed before this was introduced show "—".

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What makes Tasklist.uk different from a normal to-do list?

Tasklist.uk combines task management with AI planning, wellbeing tracking and analytics. It helps you understand both what you need to do and how you perform over time — not just a list of things to tick off.

How does the AI breakdown feature work?

The app sends your task title, description, priority, due date and subtasks to the Groq AI. It returns a plain-English summary, 4–8 numbered steps you can check off and one warning about the most likely blocker.

What is a frog task?

Your frog task is the single most important thing you need to do today. It's visually highlighted at the top of your board and should be tackled before anything else — based on Brian Tracy's Eat the Frog productivity method.

What wellbeing data is tracked?

You can track mood, happiness, energy, stress and sleep using a 1–5 rating system in the daily check-in. Optional notes can be added. Trends are charted over 14 days.

What are workspaces?

Workspaces let you separate different areas of your life — Personal and Work are created by default. Tasks, projects and bookmarks are fully isolated per workspace. Switch between them instantly from the workspace pill on the Board tab.

How do I assign a task to a teammate?

Join or create a team, then open the Collaborate tab to access your team's Collaboration Board. Create a task there and pick a teammate from the Assign To dropdown — they'll get a notification and the task appears on the shared board for everyone to see.

Can I share my board with others?

Yes — generate a read-only link from Settings. Choose to share a specific workspace or all workspaces at once. Viewers see a clean board without being able to edit anything. Revoke the link at any time.

Is Tasklist.uk free?

Yes. Tasklist.uk is completely free to use with no subscription required. Sign in with Google and get started immediately.

Is it mobile friendly?

Yes. The interface is fully responsive with a bottom navigation bar on mobile, safe-area spacing for iOS, and a long-press menu to move tasks between columns without drag and drop. The bottom nav covers Board, Calendar, Collaborate (when you're on a team) and Teams, with a More menu for Wellbeing, Stats, Saved and Settings.
