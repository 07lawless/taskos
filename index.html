🐸 The Method

Eat the frog first.
Every single day.

Brian Tracy's famous productivity principle: identify your single most important, most dreaded task — your frog — and do it first. Everything else gets easier after that.

"If it's your job to eat a frog, it's best to do it first thing in the morning. And if it's your job to eat two frogs, it's best to eat the biggest one first."
— Mark Twain
