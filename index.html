The method

Eat the frog
first thing.

The idea is simple: every day, identify your most important and difficult task — your "frog" — and do it first, before anything else.

1
Identify the one task that matters most today. Not the easiest — the most important.
2
Flag it as your Frog. The app surfaces it prominently so you can't miss it.
3
Get an AI breakdown — a clear action plan so you know exactly how to start.
4
Do it first. Everything else feels easier after the hardest thing is done.
